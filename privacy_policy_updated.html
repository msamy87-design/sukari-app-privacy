Privacy Policy for Sukari - Diabetes Management App

Last Updated: October 3, 2025

Important: Sukari is a diabetes management application that handles sensitive health information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal and health data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Health and Medical Data

  • Blood Glucose Levels: Manually entered readings and trends
  • Blood Pressure: Systolic and diastolic measurements
  • Heart Rate: Pulse measurements and monitoring data
  • Weight and BMI: Body weight tracking and calculations
  • Medication Information: Insulin doses, medication schedules, and adherence
  • Meal and Nutrition Data: Food intake, carbohydrate counting, meal photos
  • Exercise and Activity: Physical activity logs, steps, and fitness data
  • Sleep Patterns: Sleep duration and quality metrics
  • Symptoms and Notes: User-entered health observations and diary entries

1.2 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, age, gender
  • Profile Data: Diabetes type, diagnosis date, healthcare provider information
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: App interaction patterns, feature usage, session duration

1.3 Camera and Photo Data

  • Meal Photos: Images of food for nutritional analysis
  • Document Scanning: Medical documents, prescriptions, lab results
  • Barcode Scanning: Food product barcodes for nutritional information

1.4 Emergency Contact Information

  • Emergency Contacts: Names and phone numbers you designate for critical health alerts
  • Emergency Preferences: Your preferences for emergency notifications and thresholds

Note: Sukari does not collect location data or track your physical location at any time.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Health Management Purposes

  • Track and monitor your diabetes management progress
  • Provide personalized health insights and recommendations
  • Generate reports for healthcare providers
  • Send medication and appointment reminders
  • Analyze meal impact on blood glucose levels
  • Provide AI-powered health recommendations

2.2 App Functionality

  • Synchronize data across your devices
  • Backup and restore your health data
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Improve app features and user experience

2.3 Communication

  • Send important health alerts and notifications
  • Provide educational content about diabetes management
  • Share app updates and new features
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal health information to third parties.

3.1 Healthcare Providers

With your explicit consent, we may share your health data with:

  • Your designated healthcare providers
  • Medical professionals you authorize
  • Healthcare institutions you specify

3.2 Emergency Alert System

Sukari includes emergency features for critical glucose situations:

  • Critical Glucose Alerts: Automatic alerts for severe hypoglycemia (<54 mg/dL) and hyperglycemia (>400 mg/dL)
  • Emergency Contact Calling: Direct calling functionality to your designated emergency contacts
  • Emergency Services Access: Quick access to call emergency services (911)
  • Glucagon Instructions: Emergency guidance for severe hypoglycemia treatment

Important: Sukari does not automatically contact emergency services or share your location. All emergency actions require your manual initiation through the app interface.

3.3 Service Providers

We work with trusted service providers who assist with:

  • Supabase: Secure cloud database and authentication
  • AI Analysis Services: Meal recognition and health insights
  • Push Notification Services: Medication and appointment reminders
  • Analytics Providers: App performance and crash reporting

3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect the rights and safety of users
  • Investigate potential violations of our terms

4. Data Security and Protection

4.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure Authentication: Multi-factor authentication options
  • Access Controls: Strict access limitations to authorized personnel
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing security assessments and improvements
  • HIPAA-Compliant Infrastructure: Healthcare-grade security standards

4.2 Local Device Security

  • Biometric authentication (fingerprint, face recognition)
  • Device passcode protection
  • Automatic app locking
  • Secure local data storage

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Data Access and Control

  • Access: View all your stored health data
  • Export: Download your data in portable formats
  • Correction: Edit or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data

5.2 Privacy Settings

  • Control data sharing with healthcare providers
  • Manage notification preferences
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection
  • Configure emergency contact sharing

5.3 Communication Preferences

  • Customize notification types and frequency
  • Opt out of promotional communications
  • Set quiet hours for non-urgent notifications

6. Data Retention

6.1 Active Account Data

  • Health Data: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Historical Trends: Preserved to maintain continuity of care
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated data retained for service improvement

6.2 Account Deletion

  • Personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Backup copies removed within 90 days
  • Anonymized data may be retained for research purposes
  • Legal or safety-related data may be retained longer as required

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence, including:

  • United States (primary data centers)
  • European Union (backup and redundancy)
  • Other countries where our service providers operate

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including data processing agreements and adequacy decisions.

8. Children's Privacy

Sukari is not intended for children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian managing diabetes for a child:

  • You must be 18 years or older to create an account
  • You are responsible for all data entered about the child
  • You control all sharing and privacy settings
  • Special protections apply to children's health data
